Who We Are

Biospatial Healthcare is a pioneering company focused on providing innovative healthcare solutions powered by data analytics and machine learning. We aim to improve patient care, enhance emergency response systems, and streamline healthcare workflows through advanced technology.

Our Management Team

Jon Woodworth

Chief Executive Officer

Jon is a technology and business leader with 15+ years of experience in data analytics and machine learning. Prior to co-founding Biospatial, he was a principal at Signal Innovations Group (SIG), where he developed the company’s business with US defense and intelligence customers. Jon has a BS and MS in Engineering from NC State University.

Chris Lunsford

Chief Technology Officer

Chris leads technology development for Biospatial. He was previously the CTO for NCBP, where he architected the data ingestion framework that powers the Biospatial system. Chris has an MS in Electrical Engineering from Georgia Institute of Technology.

Josh Walters

Chief Operating Officer

Josh leads product development, testing, and customer technical support. He has extensive experience with large government and commercial contracts, having worked at BAE Systems as Director of SIG Products and Government Programs. Josh has a BS and MS in Electrical Engineering from NC State University.

Craig Smith

Chief Revenue Officer

Craig heads Biospatial’s growth and business development. With over 30 years of experience, he has held roles such as VP of Growth Strategy at Huron Consulting Group and President of Applied Health Analytics. Craig has BAs in Political Science and Economics from Brown University.

Jamie Gruener

Director, IT and Security

Jamie is responsible for maintaining the fleet of servers and workstations supporting the Biospatial platform. He has over 20 years of experience working with client-server technologies and has been with Amazon Web Services for over 10 years. Jamie has a BA in Philosophy from Franklin & Marshall College.

Peter Shargo

Director, Analytics

Pete leads the development of algorithms and tools that drive Biospatial’s analytic capabilities. Before joining Biospatial, he was a Principal at SIG and worked on advanced normalcy modeling and anomaly detection. Pete has an MS in Electrical Engineering from the University of Illinois.

Joe Ferrell

Director, Clinical Integration

Joe works closely with Biospatial’s data partners to identify actionable workflows. With over 30 years of experience in Emergency Medical Services, he has developed deep expertise in EMS data integration. Joe has a BS in Agricultural Education from Iowa State University and an MS in Adult Education from Capella University.

Tyler Vahan

Director, Marketing

Tyler leads Biospatial’s marketing efforts, focusing on brand awareness and campaign development. Previously, he worked at Industry Standard Research and is a graphic designer with 8 years of experience. Tyler has a BA in Communication Studies from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.
